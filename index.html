Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

ZAXIS collects information you provide directly (name, email via signup/contact form), information collected automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL), and data from third-party services including Supabase and analytics providers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To display personalized product recommendations
  • To track affiliate clicks and attribute commissions
  • To send newsletters if you've opted in
  • To improve our platform based on usage patterns

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for authentication (Supabase session), affiliate link tracking (to attribute commissions), and analytics. Affiliate cookies persist for 30–90 days depending on the partner program.

Data Security

User data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled. Passwords are never stored in plaintext — Supabase Auth handles all credential management using bcrypt.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact: legal@zaxis.kesug.com

Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: January 1, 2025

⚡ ZAXIS participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click a product link and make a purchase, we earn a commission — at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund independent research and keep the platform free.

How Affiliate Links Work

Affiliate links contain a tracking parameter that identifies ZAXIS as the referral source. If you purchase within the cookie window (typically 30–90 days), we receive a commission from the merchant. All affiliate links are tracked through our /api/click system.

Our Editorial Standards

Commission rates never influence our ratings or rankings. Products are evaluated solely on their merits. We frequently recommend products that pay lower commissions when they are genuinely the best option for our users.

Which Products Earn Commission?

Any product link on ZAXIS marked "Visit [Product]", "Try Free →", or similar is typically an affiliate link. We disclose this on every review and comparison page.
